Selected Project

Hotel Cancellation Prediction

Analyzed a surge in hotel booking cancellations from 15% to 37%, putting €4.02M in revenue at risk, by conducting exploratory and diagnostic analysis to identify key drivers of cancellations. Built a Gradient Boosting machine learning model using Python to predict high-risk bookings, achieving 77% detection accuracy. Proposed targeted interventions such as milestone-based confirmations and partial deposits, which could reduce cancellations to 20% within six months and recover over 35% of at-risk revenue.
